Plotaverse is a suite of applications that enables users/designers to animate images and videos. It was developed by Troy Christopher Plota, an award winning Photographer/Director who has been shooting Fashion and Advertising Campaigns for over twenty years.

Troy has been an award winning professional photographer for over 30 years. His work has appeared in top magazines including Vanity Fair, GQ, Rolling Stone, and many more. His advertising work has also appeared on dozens of billboards in New York’s Times Square, as well as on the Vegas strip.

When Plotaverse started out, Troy received support from Photographer/Influencer Trey Ratcliff and a handful of angel investors to kickstart his new business venture. Today, Facebook lists Plotaverse as top 5 photo apps in the market, garnering fans from across the world, including celebrities like Paris Hilton.
