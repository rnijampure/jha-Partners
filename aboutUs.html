Jha Partners is a California-based early-stage venture capital firm investing in startups and high-growth companies with bold ideas that span industries and continents. We partner with companies that demonstrate visionary entrepreneurship, strong growth rates and scalable ideas.

Our role goes far beyond providing financial resources to the founders we pick. We actively support our portfolio companies by offering them advisory and marketing support. We believe in being the ultimate growth partner to our portfolio companies, helping them soar high and touch new horizons.

Manish Jha is the Director of Jha Partners. A visionary entrepreneur himself, Manish brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table, having worked on both the entrepreneurial and the advisor side of things. He began his professional career in 1993. In 2015, he started his first company, Silvermine Group and built it to $XXmillion in revenue. He went on to establish Saveyra, a global digital marketing company with its base in India. In 2021, Manish made his directorial debut with the inception of Jha Partners.
