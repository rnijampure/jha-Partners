Sohrob Farudi & team

Tech geeks Sohrob Farudi, Ray Austin, Grant Cohen and Patrick Dees builds a virtual fan-controlled sports league for football fans worldwide.

Sohrob and his team of football fanatics have built the next-generation sports technology lab to transform how people experience football in a digitally-driven world...

Company:
Fan Controlled Football League (FCFL)
Founders:
Sohrob Farudi
Milestones:
  • FCFL signed a two-year deal with Twitch
  • Onboarded NFL stars Chad “Ochocinco” Johnson, Marshawn Lynch, and Richard Sherman, and Latin rapper Prince Royce, as team owners
  • Received investment from angel investors such as Jha Partners, Reddit co-founder Alex Ohanian and Bleacher Report co-founder Dave Finocchio

Sohrob Farudi is a lifelong football fanatic and the CEO of Fan Controlled Football League (FCFL). He teamed up with fellow football fanatics to build the first-ever professional virtual football league where the fans call the shots. The platform allows fans to control every aspect of the game - right from hiring the coach and drafting the players to calling all the plays in real-time.

When the company began its venture into the world of digital football gamification, they received a significant round of seed investment for the first three years from investors like Jha Partners among many others, which has helped them bring their vision into a reality.

FCFL is setting new paradigms in how people experience sports and are looking forward to learning more about immersive technologies that are transforming the face of gaming. By finding new technology partners and powering new experiences to fans across the country, FCFL is setting new frontiers in digital gaming.
